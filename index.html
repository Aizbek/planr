How it works
A quick tour of Planr.
📅 Opening a day
Just tap any day on the calendar to open it. Today is marked with a black circle. Past days let you tick off tasks you forgot. Future days let you plan ahead but not tick them off yet.
✅ Daily Extras vs Weekly Plans
Daily Extras are for that one day only — add them at the bottom. Weekly Plans are recurring tasks that show up on every day of the week. To add or remove weekly plans, use the Manage week button inside a day.
📊 Stats
The ◎ button shows two charts. The top one is your daily completion for a week (use ‹ › to see past weeks). The bottom one shows your full year — tap any point to jump to that week's daily view.
🎨 Colors
Green = 80%+ done · Orange = 50%+ · Red = under 50% · Gray = no plans made that day.
